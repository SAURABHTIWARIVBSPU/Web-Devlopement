Biography

Linus Torvalds is a Finnish- American software engineer who is the creator and principle developer of the Linux kernal, which is the kernal for a number of operating system. He also created the version control system. Torvalds was born on December 28,1969 in Helsinki Finland .He start study computer science at the university of Helsinki, and he developed the linux kernal as a student project in 1991. Since then, the Linux kernal has become one of the most widely used kernals in the widely used in the worlds used kernal in the worlds, powering everything from smartphones to supercomputer
